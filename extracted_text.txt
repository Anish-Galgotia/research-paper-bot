Introduction to Artificial Intelligence (AI)
Artificial Intelligence refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that
are programmed to think and act like humans. AI is a broad field of computer science
concerned with building smart machines capable of performing tasks that typically
require human intelligence.
Some common branches of AI include:
- Machine Learning
- Natural Language Processing
- Robotics
- Expert Systems
Applications of Artificial Intelligence
AI is used in a variety of fields today, including:
1. Healthcare: AI is used to predict diseases, assist in surgeries, and manage patient
data.
2. Finance: Fraud detection, algorithmic trading, and customer service bots.
3. Transportation: Self-driving cars and route optimization.
4. Education: Personalized learning, grading automation, and tutoring systems.
5. Entertainment: Recommendation systems in Netflix, YouTube, and Spotify.
